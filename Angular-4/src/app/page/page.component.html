Dragonlance

Dragonlance is a book series about 7 heroes. Sturm Brightblade, Tanis Half-elven, Caramon Majere, Raistlin Majere, Goldenmoon, Tasslehoff Burrfoot, Riverwind, Flint Fireforge. their main quest is to beat Takhisis, known as Dragon Queen. Of course they use help of other gods like Paladine, known as The Silver Dragon.
